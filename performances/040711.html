April 7, 2011
honeymoon EP release
Ian Bell and John Teske
suite for voice, guitar, and string quartet (vln, vla, vc, db)
Gallery 1412, Seattle

Songwriter Ian Bell and composer John Teske release an intimate five-song EP with guitar, voice, and modified string quartet (violin, viola, cello, double bass). The suite will be performed live at Gallery 1412 with local string players Jen Kozel, Brianna Atwell, and Maria Scherer Wilson, with Michael Owcharuk on piano.
