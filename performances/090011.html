Ian Bell and John Teske Northwest Tour

Songwriter Ian Bell and composer John Teske are taking their 'honeymoon EP' on tour this September in Bellingham, Seattle, Olympia, and Portland. Bell and Teske will present the 'honeymoon' suite and other original works with local string players in each city.

Ian Bell would like to think that his song writing is great but it might just be average, which is okay. His song writing touches on weakening familial bonds, the health of the natural world, and love. Ian is currently working on a series of pieces that he hopes will encourage people to seek a challenge in the music they consume.

John Teske is a composer, improviser and double bassist. John is currently writing chamber music for acoustic instruments while exploring new methods of composition through improvisation, inviting audiences to experience something new through sound.
