Broken Bow Ensemble performs mer for chamber orchestra

September 21, 2013
Rainier Valley Cultural Center, Seattle

September 26, 2013
Chapel Performance Space, Seattle

mer is a 40-minute piece for chamber orchestra that traces the many facets of human consciousness, awareness, and experience. Guided by local tide data, the orchestra slowly transforms sound through time, unraveling layers of pitch and rhythm.
