Ocean Love Scene In Two Keys(2004)
for violoncello and double bass

Ocean Love Scene in Two Keys was written for cellist Amelia Bowler during the Marrowstone Music Festival. The music paints a story of a boy that falls in love with a girl—but the girl loves the ocean. Heartbroken, the boy drowns himself in the ocean.
