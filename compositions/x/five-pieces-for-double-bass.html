five pieces for double bass(2011, revised 2013)

Over the course of the five pieces, the sound of the bass starts very controlled and focused, and slowly opens into a more free and improvisatory sound. Commissioned by Jayme Aumann, Chris McGill, Hannah Hoose, Tim Scearce and his daughter Grace, and India Holden.
